JAI ODISHA SOCIAL ORGANISATION

Jai Odisha is a Bhubaneswar-based voluntary social organization, established in 2012, dedicated to building a compassionate, inclusive, and empowered society. Founded under the leadership of social activist Mr. Rudra Narayan Samantaray , the organization emerged from a simple belief that every citizen has a role to play in creating a better Odisha.
Since its inception, Jai Odisha has worked across diverse areas of social welfare, community service, youth engagement, cultural exchange, and humanitarian support. The organization strives to connect individuals, institutions, and communities to address social challenges through collective action and volunteerism.
Over the years, Jai Odisha has undertaken several impactful initiatives, including support services for international students, community development programs, and humanitarian projects such as "Sesha Saathi" , a free hearse service for underprivileged families. The organization also promotes social harmony, cultural understanding, and citizen participation in public causes.
With the vision of treating Odisha as one extended family, Jai Odisha continues to create opportunities for service, foster social responsibility, and extend support to those in need. Through compassion, collaboration, and community engagement, Jai Odisha aims to contribute to a stronger, more caring, and more inclusive Odisha.
Since 2012, Jai Odisha has remained committed to its mission of serving society and inspiring citizens to actively participate in building a better future for Odisha and its people.

The objective of Jai Odisha, a voluntary youth organization based in Bhubaneswar, is to facilitate the socio-economic upliftment of the people of Odisha. The forum is dedicated to empowering local communities and improving the quality of life for vulnerable populations through targeted initiatives.
